Raising the bar for
sustaining agricultre,
through vertical farming.

At IGRO farms, we dont's just grow crops, we provides service on vertical
farming for both small and large scale farm, therefore increasing the food
security, reducing high cost of farming necessity, and making maximum
use of the available space for great gain.

Services

Offer Consultation Services: We will offer consultation services
to farmers who are interested in vertical farming. We will
educate them on the benefits of this farming method, provide
them with the necessary information on how to get started,
and help them develop a customized plan that suits
their needs and resources.

Provide Training: We will also provide training services to
farmers who want to learn more about vertical farming.
We will offer them workshops and training sessions on
the various techniques and technologies involved in
vertical farming, as well as guidance on how to
maintain and manage the farm.

Sell Vertical Farming Equipment: We will offer a range of
high-quality vertical farming equipment to our clients.
These could include vertical farming towers, hydroponic
systems, LED grow lights, and more. By offering
equipment for sale, we can help farmers set up their
farms more easily and at a lower cost.
